Components Used in LoRa Tunnel Safety System:

1. Arduino Uno (2 units) – for both transmitter and receiver nodes
2. LoRa SX1278 Modules (2 units)
3. Raspberry Pi (used for cloud interfacing with ThingSpeak)
4. DHT11 Sensor – for temperature and humidity
5. MQ-2 Sensor – for gas leakage detection
6. Buzzer – for emergency alert
7. LED – visual indicator
8. Jumper wires
9. Breadboard
10. Power Bank or 9V Battery for Arduino
